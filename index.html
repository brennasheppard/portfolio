About Me

Pop me! ✦

I am a results-driven Learning & Development professional with over a decade of experience designing and delivering impactful training solutions that drive measurable business outcomes. My journey has taken me through the worlds of instructional design, corporate training, curriculum development, and educational technology implementation—each role adding new dimensions to my expertise.

Currently serving as a Digital Learning Specialist, I partner with organizations to implement technology-enhanced learning solutions, conduct customized professional development, and create scalable training programs. What truly excites me is translating complex concepts into engaging learning experiences for adult learners across multiple delivery modalities—whether that's a virtual workshop, an interactive e-learning module, or an in-person training session.

My toolkit includes needs assessment, program design, LMS implementation, facilitation, and measurement of learning impact. I bring a unique blend of instructional expertise, project management skills, and technical proficiency to every engagement. Whether working as a trainer, consultant, instructional designer, or project lead, I am committed to creating learning solutions that align with organizational goals and deliver lasting performance improvement.

When I'm not crafting learning experiences, you'll find me exploring new EdTech tools, staying current with industry trends, and continuously expanding my own skillset—because in L&D, we never stop learning!
